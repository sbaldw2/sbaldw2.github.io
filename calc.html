MATH 1550, 1552, & 2057

MATH 1550, 1552, & 2057 – Analytic Geometry and Calculus I & II, & Multi-Dimensional Calculus III.

Courses Description - Analytic geometry, limits, derivatives, integrals. Techniques of integration, parameter equations, polar coordinates, infinite series, vectors in low dimensions; introduction to differential equations and partial derivatives. Three-dimensional analytic geometry, partial derivatives, multiple integrals.

Assignments – I: limits, derivatives, and integrals; II: integration techniques, parametric equations, polar coordinates, infinite series, vectors, begin differential equations and partial derivatives; III: partial derivatives, and multiple integrals.

Course Reflection – In these courses, I learned a great deal about calculus. Everything from the fundamentals to the more in depth, and then beginning of multi-dimensional calculus.
