CSC 2259

CSC 2259 – Discrete Structures

Course Description - Set algebra including mappings and relations; algebraic structures including semigroups and groups; elements of the theory of directed and undirected graphs; Boolean algebra and propositional logic; these structures applied to various areas of computer science.

Course Reflection – In this course, I learned set algebra mappings and relations, boolean algebra and logic, utilizing Venn diagrams, and more. I was a notetaker for LSU Disability Services for this course.
